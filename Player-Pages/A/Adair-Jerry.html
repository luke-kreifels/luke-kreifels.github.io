Career with the Royals

Selected in the expansion draft, Jerry Adair played for the Royals in their first two seasons, appearing in 126 games during their inaugural year.

Kansas City was his final major league stop, capping a 13-year career in which he also played for the Orioles, Red Sox, and White Sox, collecting over 1,000 hits.
