Career with the Royals

Acquired in a late-season August trade from the Diamondbacks, the 35-year-old Paul Abbott provided valuable innings for a 2003 Royals team that achieved over 80 wins for the first time in a decade.

Abbott, who played for six major league teams during his career (Twins, Indians, Mariners, Devil Rays, Phillies, and Royals), appeared in 11 MLB seasons from 1990 to 2004.
